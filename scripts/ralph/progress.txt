## Codebase Patterns
- `getManagerGdoTables()` in `gdoPerformanceActions.ts` queries leads by `appointmentDate` in month and groups by `assignedToId` + `funnel`
- Per-GDO metrics (leadAssegnati, percFissaggio) are displayed in the GDO card header, not as table columns, since the table is funnel-level
- Lead assignment count uses `createdAt` in month period + `isNotNull(assignedToId)` (all statuses, not just NEW)
